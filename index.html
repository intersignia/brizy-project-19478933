About Fithra

Recognized as one of the most prominent economists in Indonesia he was appointed as the official spokesperson of the Minister of Trade of the Republic of Indonesia in 2020. Fithra Faisal is widely featured in the regional and global news media coverage as a talented economic pundit. He's also appointed as the commissioner of Infra Digital Nusantara, and the executive director of Next Policy who is actively giving advice on the nation's economic issues.
